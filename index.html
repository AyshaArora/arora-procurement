FAQ

Do you work with ministries directly?

Yes, provided there’s no conflict with active employment and the work doesn’t overlap with internal responsibilities. We also support municipalities, agencies, and vendors who sell to government.

Fixed‑fee vs. hourly — how do I choose?

Fixed‑fee is best when outcomes are clear. Hourly/capped is useful for ad‑hoc help or when requirements are evolving. We’ll recommend the simplest option after discovery.

Can you start quickly?

Typical start is 1–2 weeks from a signed SOW. Rush options are sometimes available.
